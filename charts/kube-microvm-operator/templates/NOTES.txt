Lambda VM ACK Operator has been installed.

1. Verify the operator is running:
   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "kube-microvm-operator.name" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

2. Create your first MicroVM:
   cat <<EOF | kubectl apply -f -
   apiVersion: lambda.aws.amazon.com/v1alpha1
   kind: MicroVM
   metadata:
     name: my-first-vm
   spec:
     runtime: java21
     memoryMB: 512
     vcpus: 2
   EOF

3. Check MicroVM status:
   kubectl get microvms
   kubectl describe microvm my-first-vm
